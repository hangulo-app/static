How to Use:

First, click 'Play Demo.' This will ask to access your microphone, which is used to record your attempts for reading each block of text.

Once the game begins, you'll see the first block/syllable become emphasized. That is your cue to read aloud the corresponding block. Soon after, you should see your response below the block.

If your response matches, the next block becomes activated, and so on, until the end is reached.
